Import from PDF

Upload a text-based PDF with clear headings like Words: and Definitions:. The first word will be matched with the first definition, the second word with the second definition, and so on. For best results, the PDF should only contain the vocabulary words and their matching definitions. Do not include extra headings, instructions, page labels, answer keys, notes, or unrelated text between the Words section and the Definitions section.

Scanned image PDFs cannot be read unless OCR is added later.
